Comparing 2010s YA and 2020s YA
2010s 2020s
Time Period 2006-2013 2018-2023
Main Characters Mary Sues that are perfect at everything with maybe social awkwardness. Blank slates to allow teen girls to project onto them. More capable and self assured. Doesn't need a man to protect her. Still blank slates and Mary Sues.
Love Interests Love triangles between the blond childhood friend and the black haired broody possessive bad boy. Both toxic options. Protective and possessive but supportive of crimes. Less love triangles, more enemies to lovers. Sometimes just mean and cruel but “it’s because he loves her”.
Side characters Main diversity can be found in the best friend who's only there to make the main character seem better or to add drama. More diverse overall and they tend to have more ofan important role in the story than just making the main character look better.
Genres Supernatural romance or dystopia. Fantasy, romantasy, or fairytale retellings.
Storylines Taking down the government or falling in love with a supernatural being that puts you at risk by simply loving them. Political intrigue with a royal family or a quest plot.
Settings Urban fantasy settings or postapocalyptic dystopian settings. High fantasy mostly. Occasionally an urban fantasy setting.
